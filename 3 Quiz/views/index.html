Quiz
This quizz consists of two parts. One where you have one music to hear, and several partitions to choose from, and one that is the opposite, i.e. several music clips and one partition.
You will first have 4 questions from one part, then 3 questions from the other part. So don't be confused if the exercises seems different after a while, the base concept stays the same : trying to match music clip(s) and music sheet(s) together. The different choices you have are marked with alphabet symbols (a,b,c, or d).
