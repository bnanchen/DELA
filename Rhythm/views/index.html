Rhythm Exercise
Hello, and welcome to our little lesson on Music Reading ! You learn better by practicing, so we will start directly with a small rhythm exercise before giving you some formal knowledge via a video explanation. Please read carefully the paragraph below.

A video will be shown on the screen. It is an extract of a concert from The White Stripes. Your goal is to tap in rhythm (i.e. at the same time) with the kick of the drums. You can press any letter of your keyboard to tap the rhythm. You will easily recognize this famous riff, it will be played two times before the drum kick starts, so be vigilant. After a while, the song will slowly fades out, you need to keep on taping on your keyboard at the same pace, just like if you still heard the drum kick. You have only one try, so make it count !
